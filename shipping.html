Frequently Asked Questions

How long does international shipping take?

International shipping times vary depending on the destination and shipping method. Air freight typically takes 3-7 business days, while ocean freight can take 20-45 days depending on the route.

Do you provide insurance for shipped items?

Yes, we offer optional insurance coverage for all shipments. The cost is typically a percentage of the declared value of your items.

How do I track my shipment?

Once your shipment is processed, you will receive a tracking number that you can use on our website or through our customer service to monitor your shipment's progress.
