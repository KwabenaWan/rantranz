Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, a major credit card in your name, and proof of insurance. International renters may need additional documentation.

Is there a mileage limit on rentals?

Most of our rentals come with unlimited mileage within the state. For out-of-state travel, specific mileage packages are available.

Can I add an additional driver to my rental?

Yes, additional drivers can be added to your rental agreement for a small fee. They must meet the same eligibility requirements as the primary driver.

What is your cancellation policy?

Reservations can be canceled up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time without penalty. Late cancellations may incur a fee
