About Ran Tranz

Your trusted partner in procurement and shipping solutions

Our Story

Ran Tranz is a premier procurement and shipping company based in Chicago, Illinois, specializing in promoting trade through imports and exports. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to providing tailored logistics solutions for individuals, businesses, and institutions.

Our mission is to leverage effective supply-chain systems and transportation equipment to deliver exceptional service to our clients. We pride ourselves on our expertise in international trade, particularly between the United States and West Africa.

At Ran Tranz, we understand that each client has unique needs. That's why we offer customized solutions that address specific requirements, ensuring satisfaction and building long-term relationships with our customers.

Our Journey

Explore the key milestones that have shaped Ran Tranz into the global logistics leader it is today.

2010

Company Founded Founding

Ran Tranz was established in Chicago with a vision to connect U.S. and West African markets through efficient logistics solutions.

Initial team of 5 employees

Our founder, Robert F. Nuamah, identified a significant gap in the logistics market between the United States and West Africa. With an initial investment and a small dedicated team, Ran Tranz began operations from a modest office in Chicago's business district.

2011

First Office Expansion Milestone

Moved to a larger office space to accommodate our growing team and expanding operations.

Team grew to 12 employees

Within just one year of operations, our rapid growth necessitated a move to a larger facility. This expansion allowed us to hire additional staff with specialized expertise in international logistics and trade regulations, setting the foundation for our future success.

2012

First International Shipment Milestone

Successfully completed our first major international shipment, establishing our presence in the global logistics market.

20 vehicles shipped to Ghana

This landmark shipment marked our entry into international logistics operations. The successful delivery of 20 vehicles to Ghana established our reputation for reliability and efficiency in cross-continental shipping.

2013

First Industry Recognition Achievement

Received our first industry award for excellence in international logistics services.

"Emerging Logistics Provider of the Year"

This recognition from the International Trade Association validated our business model and approach to logistics. The award highlighted our commitment to quality service and innovative solutions in connecting American and West African markets.

2015

Expanded Services Expansion

Added car procurement and shipping services to our portfolio, catering to the growing demand for American vehicles in West Africa.

Service portfolio doubled

Responding to market demand, we expanded our service offerings to include comprehensive vehicle procurement services. This strategic expansion allowed us to provide end-to-end solutions for clients seeking quality American vehicles for import to West African markets.

2018

Dealer Network Established Expansion

Created a comprehensive network of dealership partners across the United States, enhancing our procurement capabilities.

50+ dealer partnerships

We established formal partnerships with over 50 dealerships across the United States, giving us unprecedented access to a wide range of vehicles at competitive prices. This network became a cornerstone of our procurement strategy, allowing us to source specific vehicles quickly and efficiently.

2019

Quality Excellence Award Achievement

Recognized for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction in logistics services.

98% customer satisfaction rate

This prestigious industry award recognized our dedication to maintaining the highest standards of service quality. Our customer-centric approach and attention to detail in every shipment earned us this recognition, setting a new benchmark in the logistics industry.

2020

Digital Transformation Innovation

Implemented advanced digital tracking and management systems to improve service efficiency and customer experience.

30% efficiency increase

Our digital transformation initiative revolutionized our operations. By implementing cutting-edge tracking systems and a comprehensive digital management platform, we achieved a 30% increase in operational efficiency and significantly enhanced our customer experience through real-time shipment tracking.

2021

Sustainability Initiative Achievement

Launched our eco-friendly shipping program to reduce carbon footprint and promote sustainable logistics.

15% reduction in carbon emissions

Our commitment to environmental responsibility led to the development of our Green Shipping Initiative. By optimizing shipping routes, implementing fuel-efficient practices, and partnering with eco-conscious carriers, we've significantly reduced our carbon footprint while maintaining service excellence.

2022

Mobile App Launch Innovation

Released our mobile application to provide clients with on-the-go access to our services and shipment tracking.

10,000+ downloads in first month

Our mobile app represents a significant step forward in client convenience. With features like real-time tracking, instant quote requests, and document uploads, clients can now manage their shipments from anywhere in the world. The app's intuitive interface and comprehensive functionality have received overwhelmingly positive feedback from our user base.

2023

Global Expansion Expansion

Expanded our operations to include additional international markets, further solidifying our position as a global logistics provider.

5 new countries served

Our strategic expansion into five additional countries marked a significant milestone in our global growth strategy. By establishing operations in key markets across Africa and Europe, we've created a truly international network that enables seamless logistics solutions for our clients worldwide.

2024

AI-Powered Logistics Innovation

Implemented artificial intelligence solutions to optimize routing, pricing, and delivery predictions.

25% improvement in delivery time accuracy

Our investment in AI technology has revolutionized our logistics operations. By leveraging machine learning algorithms to analyze historical data and predict optimal shipping routes, we've significantly improved delivery time accuracy and reduced costs. This cutting-edge approach positions us at the forefront of technological innovation in the logistics industry.
