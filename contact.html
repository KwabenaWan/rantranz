What areas do you provide shipping services to?

We provide shipping services both locally within the United States and internationally, with specialized expertise in shipping to West Africa.

How can I track my shipment?

Once your shipment is processed, you will receive a tracking number via email that you can use to monitor your shipment's progress through our online tracking system.

What types of vehicles do you offer through your dealer services?

Through our dealer services, we can help you locate and purchase a wide range of vehicles, including luxury cars, SUVs, trucks, and economy models, both new and pre-owned.

How much does international shipping cost?

International shipping costs vary based on destination, size, weight, and shipping method. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific requirements.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, a major credit card, and proof of insurance. International customers may need an International Driving Permit depending on their country of origin.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For international transactions, we also work with secure wire transfer services.

Do you offer financing options for vehicle purchases?

Yes, we work with several financial institutions to provide competitive financing options for both domestic and international clients. Contact our dealer services team for more information.

Can I extend my rental period after I've already rented the vehicle?

Yes, rental extensions are possible subject to vehicle availability. Please contact our rental department at least 24 hours before your scheduled return to arrange an extension.
